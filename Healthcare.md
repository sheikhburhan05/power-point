## Topic

Artificial intelligence transforming healthcare delivery, diagnostics, and patient outcomes.

## The Problem

- Healthcare systems face rising costs, clinician burnout, and growing patient volumes.
- Diagnostic errors contribute to tens of thousands of preventable deaths annually in the US alone.
- Radiologists review thousands of images per year; fatigue increases miss rates.
- Rural and underserved areas lack access to specialist expertise.
- Patient data is fragmented across EHRs, labs, and imaging systems — hard to synthesize at the point of care.
- Administrative burden consumes up to 30% of clinician time.

## Our Solution

- AI-powered diagnostic support that flags anomalies in medical imaging with high accuracy.
- Clinical decision support that surfaces relevant patient history and evidence-based recommendations.
- Automated documentation and coding assistance to reduce admin load on physicians.
- Predictive analytics for early intervention — readmission risk, sepsis, deterioration alerts.
- Unified patient intelligence layer that connects EHR, imaging, and lab data in real time.
